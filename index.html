WHAT / CLAUDE20

CLAUDE-20 is a Base-native inscription mint protocol with a fixed supply of 21 million tokens.

Public mint allocation is capped at 15 million CLAUDE, while 6 million tokens remain reserved for liquidity provisioning and protocol launch mechanics.

Mint transactions generate an on-chain JSON inscription which tracks mint progress and public supply allocation.
